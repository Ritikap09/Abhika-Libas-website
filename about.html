About Us

Welcome to Abhika Libas! We specialize in designer Kurtis with modern styles and traditional comfort. Our mission is to provide stylish outfits at affordable prices.

Meet the Founder

Hello! I am Ritika Pathak, the founder of Abhika Libas. With a deep passion for fashion and creativity, I started this brand to bring stylish, comfortable, and affordable clothing to women who love expressing themselves through fashion.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a trusted destination for women’s ethnic and modern wear, where fashion meets comfort and affordability.

Mission: To deliver high-quality, trendy, and elegant kurtis that suit every occasion — whether it’s daily wear, festive celebrations, or casual outings.

What We Offer

We specialize in new and trendy fashion kurtis designed for every woman, for everywhere and every occasion. Each product is crafted with attention to detail, blending modern trends with cultural elegance.
